🏏 The Symphony of Cricket

Under the sun on emerald ground,
Where cheers and dreams together resound,
A pitch lies calm in the center stage,
Witness to glory, skill, and rage.

The crowd awakens with hopeful eyes,
As the bowler begins his rhythmic stride,
Leather in hand, focus in sight,
Ready to battle with bat and might.

The batsman waits with patient grace,
Guarding the stumps, steady in place,
Bat raised high like a warrior’s sword,
Prepared for the challenge the bowler has stored.

The bowler runs with thunderous pace,
Determination etched on his face,
Arm swings over, the ball takes flight,
A red comet blazing in morning light.

The batsman moves with perfect art,
Timing and instinct guide his heart,
A crack of willow splits the air,
The ball goes soaring everywhere.

Across the field it swiftly flies,
Past diving hands and hopeful tries,
The crowd erupts with joyful cry,
As the ball sails proudly to the sky.

Fielders scatter like hunters keen,
Chasing the ball across the green,
Every step a race with fate,
Every second sealing the state.

Sometimes the bowler wins the war,
A spinning trap or seaming roar,
The stumps explode, the bails take flight,
A moment of triumph shining bright.

Other times the bat prevails,
Sending the ball beyond the rails,
A boundary cheers, a six divine,
Like fireworks in perfect time.

But cricket is more than runs and fame,
More than records or winning a game,
It’s patience, courage, respect, and pride,
A gentleman’s spirit standing beside.

From dusty streets where children play,
With taped-up balls in light of day,
To grand arenas filled with song,
The love of cricket grows lifelong.

Heroes rise with bat and ball,
Legends echo in stadium hall,
Their stories whispered through the years,
In every cheer and every tear.

For cricket is poetry in motion,
A dance of strategy and devotion,
Where every match writes history anew,
Under skies of endless blue.

And when the final over is done,
When fading light replaces sun,
The game remains in hearts that know,
Cricket’s magic will always glow. 🏏✨
